About SELECT

With over 20 years of experience, we've been providing top-quality electrical services to homeowners and businesses in the Sparksville area.

Our Story

SELECT was founded in 2005 by Michael Johnson, a master electrician with a vision to provide reliable and honest electrical services to the community. What started as a one-man operation has grown into a team of 15 skilled electricians serving the entire Sparksville metropolitan area.

Our mission is simple: to deliver safe, efficient, and high-quality electrical solutions that meet our clients' needs while exceeding industry standards. We take pride in our work and stand behind every job we complete.

All of our electricians are fully licensed, insured, and continuously trained on the latest electrical technologies and safety protocols. We're committed to staying at the forefront of the industry to provide you with the best service possible.
